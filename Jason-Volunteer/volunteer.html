Volunteer

Helping animals more than anything else!

Volunteers make a significant contribution to the Montreal SPCAâ€™s operations. Their support and dedication are essential to the accomplishment of its mission. These valued allies allow us to offer a range of services that go far beyond the traditional role attributed to animal shelters. The SPCA is looking for people who are willing to dedicate time to helping animals.

Are you already a volunteer at the Montreal SPCA or looking to create an login account? Please plan your visits to the shelter here.

Requirements:

  • Be 18 years old or older
  • Be able to volunteer a minimum of three consecutive hours per week
  • Be able to work on a fixed schedule (at the same time each week)
  • Be able to commit at least 6 months of volunteering
  • Be fluent in French and English (spoken)

*It is recommended that you are reachable by your email.

Application

Are you under 18 years old?

*Contact our parter ENGAGE at info@engageanimal.org

Are you 18 years old or over?

*After receiving your application, we will contact you as soon as we can and share any openings by email.

**You will need to pay a $20 administration fee and will be provided with a uniform.
